Experiment 4

White Sauce for Pasta

Ingredients

  • 2 Tablespoon butter
  • 2 tablespoons margarine
  • 3 tablespoons all-purpose floor
  • 1 cup favourite vegies
  • 1 1/2 cups boiling water
  • 1 cup 2% milk
  • ground white pepper to taste

Directions

Step 1
Gather the Indredients.

Step 2
Melt butter and margarine in a saucepan over medium-low heat.

Step 3
Add flour and stir until well blended, 1 to minutes.

Step 4
Continue to cook and stir until thickend and lightly browned, about 5 minutes..

Step 5
Increase the heat to medium and whisk in water until smooth.

Step 6
Stir in milk; cook and stir until hot and thickened, 2 to 3 more minutes.

Step 7
Season with white pepper.
